About Me

I am a graduate student specializing in intelligent wearable sensing and human motion analysis. My research and project experience cover wearable sensor data processing, deep learning modeling, gait recognition, abnormal gait detection, and cross-subject experimental evaluation. I work extensively with IMU sensors, plantar pressure data, biomechanical prior features, and deep learning models, with a strong focus on edge device deployment and real-world applications.
